About Us


Femfluid is a brainchild of collaboration between LubgrafSynoils Private limited and Charotar University of Science and Technology (CHARUSAT). Our Knowledge stems from our core collaborators who specialize in design, Synthesis and mechanics.

To visualize Femfluids mission our dedicated team has established a strong research and development wing at CHARUSAT. Our state of the art facility is equipped for performance as well as application driven instruments that simulate real world’s conditions.

Femfluid’s knowledge of advanced science helps in bring forth various vibration solutions, Smart fluid and nano materials that are beneficial to range of industries.

Femfluid provides customized solutions to various industrial problems. Product ranges are MR fluids, MR grease, MR polishing fluids, MR based dampers for automotive, heavy vehicles, aerospace, medical and civil industries.
